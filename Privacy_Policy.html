Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Movie Hub App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles your information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Overview

Movie Hub App does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

This application is designed to provide movie details, trailers (via YouTube links), and public data via APIs such as The Movie Database (TMDb). It does not require any form of login or user account.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

  • No names, emails, or contact details.
  • No location data or user-tracking information.
  • No usage analytics or device-specific identifiers are recorded or stored by us.

The app may retrieve publicly available movie data using external APIs like TMDb. These services may have their own privacy policies which we do not control.

Third-Party Services

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Security

As we do not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of personal data breaches from our side. However, we encourage users to use secure devices and networks while browsing external links.
